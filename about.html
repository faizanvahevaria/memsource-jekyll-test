ABOUT US

Memsource was founded in 2010 and has become a leader in cloud-based translation technology. We are internationally recognized for providing an easy-to-use, yet fully-featured translation tool and management system for the entire translation community of buyers, agencies, and translators. Headquartered in Prague with offices around the world, we value working with businesses and professionals who share our passion for languages and the technology that enables the world to communicate.
